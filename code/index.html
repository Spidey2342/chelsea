Climate Change in Ghana: Quick Facts

Temperature Rise

Ghana's average temperature has increased by 1°C since 1960 and could rise by 1.5-3°C by 2050.

Rainfall Patterns

Rainfall has decreased by 20% in some regions while becoming more intense and erratic.

Sea Level Rise

Sea levels are rising at 3.4mm per year, threatening coastal communities like Accra.
